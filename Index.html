Saint Martin Island

Diverse Culture: Experience a blend of French and Dutch influences, with stunning beaches, delicious cuisine, and vibrant local traditions. Currency and Language: Use Euros on the French side, English and French are spoken widely. About US

Nestled in South Asia, Bangladesh beckons with its stunning Ganges-Brahmaputra delta, a mosaic of fertile plains and waterways. Dhaka, the bustling capital, showcases a blend of historical treasures like Lalbagh Fort and modern urban life. The world's longest natural sea beach graces Cox's Bazar, while Sylhet's tea-carpeted hills and Rangamati's serene lakes provide picturesque escapes. Sample the flavorful Bengali cuisine, from aromatic biryanis to sweet treats like roshogolla. Immerse yourself in local life by partaking in village tourism, embracing the warm hospitality and traditional crafts. Experience vibrant festivals and respect customs for a truly enriching journey through this diverse nation.
